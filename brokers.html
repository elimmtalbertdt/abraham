Trading View

TradingView is a powerful platform for charting and social trading, not a traditional broker. It offers advanced chart tools, real-time data, and a huge library of indicators. Users can share ideas, follow traders, and analyse markets collaboratively. Although it is not a broker itself, it integrates with brokers such as FOREX.com for direct trading. It covers stocks, forex, crypto, futures, and more across global markets. Overall: a charting powerhouse, not a broker, but with strong broker integrations.

Plus500

Plus500 is a global CFD broker that allows users to trade a wide range of assets, including forex, stocks, indices, commodities, and cryptocurrencies. It offers a user-friendly platform with zero commissions, fixed spreads, and risk management tools like stop-loss. Plus500 is regulated in multiple jurisdictions and provides a free demo account, making it accessible for both beginner and experienced traders.

FTX

FTX was a major cryptocurrency exchange known for offering derivatives, spot trading, and tokenized assets with low fees and advanced features. Founded by Sam Bankman-Fried, it quickly rose in popularity before collapsing in 2022 due to alleged fraud, misuse of customer funds, and lack of internal controls. The company filed for bankruptcy, and Bankman-Fried was later convicted on multiple criminal charges.
